Biography of Jim Carrey

About

Jim Carrey is a canadian-born actor and comedian. This page will talk about his early career, most notable movies, and his current endeavors.

Early Career

Carrey did not start his career as an actor. He originally focused on stand-up comedy. In the late 70s and early 80s, Jim Carrey rose to some prominence in the comedy community. He worked at The Comedy Story in Los Angeles. It was at The Comedy Store that he was discovered by proclaimed comedian Rodney Dangerfield, who signed Carrey to open his tour performances.

Jim Carrey has had a long interest in film and television and his friendship with fellow comedian Damon Wayans, landed Carrey a role on a sketch comedy television show called In Living Color. This began Carrey's long and prevelant acting career. His first major break through in film was when he was cast as the lead character in Ace Ventura: Pet Detective. This film was the catalyst to Carrey's major career in film. His fame would continue to grow as he became cast in more and more notable movies.

Notable Movies

  • Ace Ventura: Pet Detective
  • Dumb and Dumber
  • The Mask
  • The Truman show
  • How the Grinch Stole Christmas
  • Man on the Moon

Current Endeavors

Currently, Jim Carrey has talked about stepping away from acting. His last project was Sonic the Hedgehog 2, but he has focused on other projects besides acting in recent years. These projects include:

  1. Publishing a surrealist novel titled Memoirs and Misinformation.
  2. Painting
  3. Sculpting
  4. Spending time with friends
